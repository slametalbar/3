Pujiang Town station

Pujiang Town (simplified Chinese: 浦江镇; traditional Chinese: 浦江鎮; pinyin: Pǔjiāng Zhèn) is a station on Line 8 of the Shanghai Metro. This station is part of the southern extension of Line 8 and opened on July 5, 2009.

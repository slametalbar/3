Krzywiec, Łódź Voivodeship

Krzywiec [ˈkʂɨvʲɛt͡s] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Aleksandrów Łódzki, within Zgierz County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south of Aleksandrów Łódzki, 12 km (7 mi) south-west of Zgierz, and 13 km (8 mi) west of the regional capital Łódź.

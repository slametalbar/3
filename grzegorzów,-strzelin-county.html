Grzegorzów, Strzelin County

Grzegorzów [ɡʐɛˈɡɔʐuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kondratowice, within Strzelin County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 6 km (4 mi) north-east of Kondratowice, 9 km (6 mi) north-west of Strzelin, and 35 km (22 mi) south of the regional capital Wrocław.
